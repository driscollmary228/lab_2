School Histroy


In 1822 the second leavers of the sixth standard had no catholic secondary school to attend and even thougth promises were made that the secondary school would be built ,nothing happen till 1822 when it was decided to use the middle floor of the new building at Magburaka, Bo road Street for new secondary school. In january 1922 advertisement appeared in the weekly news that the new Driscoll's Accademy would be opened on the 6th February 1922 on Monday
